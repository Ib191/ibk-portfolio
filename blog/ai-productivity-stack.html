Building a Personal Productivity Stack with Generative AI

Published August 2, 2025 8 min read

My system funnels inputs from meetings, research, and ideas into a trusted workflow. AI keeps everything summarized, actionable, and searchable.

Calendar blocks are generated from a daily stand-up prompt I run each morning.

Finished tasks trigger recaps that update teammates on Slack automatically.
