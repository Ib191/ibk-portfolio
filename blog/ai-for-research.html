Research Faster with Agentic AI Flows

Published September 1, 2025 7 min read

I built an AI analyst that monitors industries for clients, drafts summaries, and suggests follow-up questions I can review in minutes.

Context ingestion

Every brief loads previous memos, CRM notes, and competitor data into the agent.

Multi-step reasoning

The agent calls a research API, runs custom classifiers, and proposes next steps.

Human-in-the-loop

I approve or tweak the plan. The agent then drafts a memo and updates Notion.

Architecture snapshot

  • Orchestration: LangChain on an AWS Lambda function.
  • Memory: Pinecone vector DB with 6-month retention.
  • Evaluation: Weekly review prompts and a manual QA checklist.
