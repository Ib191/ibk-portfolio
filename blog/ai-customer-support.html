How AI Assistants Make Customer Support Scalable

Published July 12, 2025 6 min read

TL;DR ? Pair an AI-first triage system with detailed playbooks for human agents. The result is faster answers, happier customers, and clean feedback loops for continuous improvement.

01. Map the journey

We started by charting the top 30 support scenarios, grouped by complexity. Simple FAQs moved to the virtual assistant, while nuanced issues stayed with people.

02. Train with real transcripts

Feeding anonymized conversations into the large language model gave it context. We generated response templates with reinforcement from support leads.

03. Close the human loop

Agents capture outcomes with a single click. That feedback updates the assistant?s suggestions weekly, ensuring the system keeps learning.

Metrics after 60 days

45% automation

Nearly half of incoming chats resolved without human intervention.

+18 CSAT points

Customers rated answers more helpful thanks to consistent knowledge bases.

2x agent focus time

Specialists handled fewer repetitive questions and invested in complex cases.

Curious about the tech stack? We used Intercom, Make, and an Azure OpenAI deployment with custom prompts stored in Airtable.
